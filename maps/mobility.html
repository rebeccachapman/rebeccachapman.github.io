LEEP Research Project, Select Figures

This project is related to the broad topic of urban economic performance and schooling. This work attempts to account for patterns of spatial and temporal mobility, which is for the most part absent from current literature. This study develops several approaches to describing mobility of these students drawing upon a range of techniques in Geographical Information Science. Mobility refers to the movement of students between schools and districts for reasons other than grade promotion. See the poster product here!
