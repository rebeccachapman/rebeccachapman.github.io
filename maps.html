This area is dedicated to completed projects related to Geographic Information Science. I would also like to share a few of my favorite links and tools below.

COLORBREWER
Cartography resource. Useful to generate print-friendly and colorblind safe color schemes.

GEODA
Free, open source software program. Excellent for exploratory spatial data analysis.

CARTODB
Free mapping and visualizations. Good for small-batch geocoding or creating a web map.
